About Us

Sammy's Nest is a personal student project created to improve event and organization discovery at Rice University. The project is open-source and available on Github. Feel free to explore the code and contribute!

Sammy's Nest consolidates events from Rice Events and OwlNest in one interface. Out goal is to make it accessible and simple for you to:

  • Discover events relevant to your academic interests
  • Find organizations that match your major(s) and communiities
  • Never miss free food or development opportunities again

Behind the scenes, Sammy's Nest regularly ingests events, maps organizations to academic fields, and lets you personalize what you see based on your preferences.
